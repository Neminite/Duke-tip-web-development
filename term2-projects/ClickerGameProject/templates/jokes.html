    THE FINAL RIDDLE:

You're standing in a room with two doors. One door leads to your freedom, and the other leads to your immediate doom. One robot guards each of the doors. One robot will always tell the truth, and the other will always lie. You have one question to figure out which door to exit through, but you don't know which robot is which. What question should you ask?

    Which door would the other robot tell me is the door to my freedom?

Both the robot that tells you the truth and the robot that always lies will tell you the door that will lead to your doom. You can then choose the other door.
