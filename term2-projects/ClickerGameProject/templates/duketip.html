    Duke Tip

    The nerdiest happiest place on earth.

    Welcome to the Duke Tip page. Our class, Web Applications, was housed at Trinity University. It's a beautiful campus filled with fun and intelligent people.

    TIP stands for Talent Identification Program. Students at TIP have taken either the ACT os SAT and have been accepted into the esteemed program. Because of this application process, everyone at TIP has a love of learning.

    Trinity Campus, Term 2, 2016 has a lot of amazing classes available. The classes are: Modern Medicine, Mock Trial, Beyond Baker Street, BioChem, Speculative Fiction, Engineering, Math Problem Solving, Psychology, Algebra 2, and of course, Web Applications.

    Duke TIP has a big, wonderful staff. We have teachers to lead our classes, TAs to assist the teachers, RCs to keep all the campers in line, and a wonderful matinence and kitchen staff to help with the rest.

    Duke TIP is an amazing program and an amazing experience.

    Becca's favorite parts of TIP were spending time with her awesome RC Vanshika and RC group, making horrible puns with her roommate, and taking a lot of creepy totally normal photos of everyone for this website.

    Grant's favorite parts of TIP were the Web Applications class and making websites along with RC group night with his RC Josh.

    Dylan's favorite parts of TIP were RC group night and Web Applications. He really enjoyed spending time with his RC Aisha, as well as spending time with his wonderful class.
