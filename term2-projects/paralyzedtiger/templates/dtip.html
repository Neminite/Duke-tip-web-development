About Duke Tip

Semester Two at Trinity University

Duke TIP is a program for gifted children from grades 6-12. We get to stay in collage dorms and get to take advanced classes. We are staying at Trinity University in San Antonio, Texas. There are a variety of different classes for every kid. They have, Algebra II, Speculative Fiction, Art and Technology, Modern Medicine, Biological and Chemical Sciences, Psychology, Engineering, Mock Trial, Math Problem Solving, Beyond Baker's Street, and of course, Web Application (the best class). This camp gives you a look at the college campus and a glance at the proffesion that your class studies. The students also get to interact with others who have the same interest as them and make some life-long friends. We go out on the town , to dances, and to many fun activities. Every semester there are two big competitions Tipsync and Tigerfest. Tipsync is a lipsync competition where each RC group gets to create a lipsync preformance. This semester Lucie's group won Tipsync, Meredith's group got second, and Vanfhika's group got third. Tigerfest is a competitive field day where RC groups are combined to make for teams (Red, Blue, Green, and Grellow(Grey + Yellow)). Tigerfest has alot of fun games such as cheezy-puff toss, Rock Tree Bridge, and Day in the life of a Tipster. The red team won by a landslide of 40 pt. coming in second was the grellow team with 28 pt. Third was the green team with 26 pt. Last place was The Blue team with 20 pt. This camp has been a great experience and we hope to come back again. ALEX, AARON, AND LUCY ARE THE BEST RCs!!!!!!!!
