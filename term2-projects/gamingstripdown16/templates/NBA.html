NBA 2k

NBA 2k is another one of my favorite games. I like NBA 2k because I play basketball myself. There is only one mode that I like. It is MyCareer. It is similar to the carreer mode in Fifa. But you career starts from high school. You also get to be drafted on a team. Sadly, you rarely get drafted on your team of choice. Infact, the game doesn't even ask you. Plus it is a lot more intense. Even though that is my favorite mode, I ocassionally play season and GM mode because you get to play as any team. My favorite team on 2k are the Cleveland Caveliers.
