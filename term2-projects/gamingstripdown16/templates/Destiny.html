Destiny is a game about fighting away "The Darkness" Your characters, who are called guardians, fight off several different races of aliens, named The Hive, The Fallen, The Vex, and The Cabal.



There have been many different expansions for the game, which, unlike the original game, have a theme based off of one of the races. The first expansion to Destiny was called The Dark Below, and was hive themed. The second expansion to Destiny was called House Of Wolves, and was Fallen Themed. The third expansion to Destiny was called The Taken King, and was hive themed.




The main endgame activities, called raids, are when six different players go into the depths of the enemy lines, and take out a major leader or enemy of the Vanguard, which is the group that organizes and leads the guardians into battle. The first raid is The Vault of Glass, from original Destiny (against the Vex), where you fight a giant robot called atheon. The second raid is Crota's End, from The Dark Below (against the hive), where you fight a large monstrous creature called Crota. and Kings Fall, from The Taken King (against the Hive), where you fight Crota's father, Oryx.
